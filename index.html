My Experiences

Multidisciplinary Design Team

In January of 2020, I joined a design team for class credit through my university. A large part of our project is the development of a web application. Essentially, the app allows users to draw a picture, then generates a piece of music based on the colors, shapes, and position of brush strokes in the drawing. After a semester of work, I was offered a summer fellowship to continue work on this project, specifically on the web app. I participated in this fellowship for six weeks in the spring of 2020. In that time, I worked on the design and development of the app's user interface. I also assisted with the creation of the algorithm that transformed the drawing into music. Web development was fairly new to me, so I spent this time learning new skills and immediately applying them to the project. To complete the design successfully, it was necessary to make quick decisions regarding which features to prioritize. When making these decisions, I remained calm under pressure and used my creative thinking skills to develop an easy-to-use and aesthetically pleasing interface. The app is currently going through user testing and can be found here.

General Motors Internship

In the summer of 2020, I was an intern at General Motors on the Vehicle Systems Cybersecurity team. My main task was the development of a program that extracts, filters, and analyzes vital data from error log files, condensing millions of lines of data into readable error reports of a few hundred lines long. This program is currently being used in multiple manufacturing plants in Michigan. Because my internship was mainly virtual, my work was very self-directed. While working from home, I used my time efficiently and remained ahead of schedule. When I had space in my calendar, I reached out to my superiors to get involved with additional projects. One of these projects was the redesigning of a safety awareness SharePoint site, which is currently being used to deliver vital safety information to hundreds of GM employees.
